Why this practice

Holistic care exists, but it is rare and scattered. Usually it is delivered by one practitioner trying to cover everything. The Whole Woman is built differently — three senior specialists under one roof, sharing one method and one written record per client. What one of us sees, all three see. A woman doesn't have to repeat her story.

The approach

Her whole life
— seen in one place

A woman's adult life has arcs the system is not built to follow. Fertility concerns at thirty-two become pregnancy care at thirty-four. Postnatal hormones become cycle questions become perimenopausal symptoms. Stress load from one decade becomes the thyroid question of the next. Each stage is treated by a different clinician — often a different clinic — and the thread between them is lost.

Our approach is continuous, not episodic. Every client begins with a Blueprint — a full written picture of her health, built in conversation with one of our practitioners. From that document, the care that follows is drawn from whichever practitioner is right for the moment. Lifestyle medicine and menopause from Dr A. Gynaecological investigation from Dr B. Acupuncture and functional medicine from Lora. One client. One record. Three specialists. No seams.

The shared method

Every client
begins with a Blueprint

Your Health Masterpiece

The written picture
of your whole health

The Blueprint is a wide-ranging consultation and a written document. Your health chronology, cycle history, medications, previous treatments, stressors, and goals — mapped in one place, in conversation with one of our practitioners.

Once the picture exists, it is shared with the rest of the collective with your consent. Dr A does not need a separate intake to begin a lifestyle medicine consultation. Dr B does not start gynaecological investigation with a blank page. The thread of your care is continuous from the first consultation onward.
